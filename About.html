ABOUT US

---Who We Are---

Temini Hair Tribe is a fast growing brand aimed at educating and enlightening the African woman and man and how they can love and care for their hair, wearing it with confidence and elegance. With its very strong online presence and availability, Temini Hair Tribe has a virtual hair community of over 1000 individuals who are constantly being enlightened on everything that has to do with hair care. The hair communities which run separately for both guys and ladies is one that provides support for participants who want to take their hair journey seriously while also learning from one another as well. Temini Hair Tribe has also successfully Co pioneered 4 other different hair communities, thereby increasing the enlightened audience on how to care for the African kind of hair. With herbs, natural and organic products, we provide hair care services to clients with little or no pain. We currently operate in Osun within an hair outlet, where we meet the hair needs of our clients. At Temini Hair Tribe, we believe that every hair matters and deserves to be treated right.
